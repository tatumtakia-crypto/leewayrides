Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request transportation?

Complete our online booking calendar or contact Leeway Rides by phone or text. Please provide your pickup address, destination, date, time, passenger count, and trip type.

How far in advance should I schedule?

Advance scheduling is strongly recommended. Same-day requests may be considered based on driver availability, but transportation is not guaranteed until confirmed.

Is booking through the calendar a confirmed ride?

Online bookings and requests are subject to confirmation and availability by our team.

Do you provide one-way and round-trip transportation?

Yes. Customers may request one-way, round-trip, recurring, local, or out-of-town transportation.

Do you provide medical transportation?

We provide scheduled, non-emergency transportation for ambulatory passengers traveling to medical appointments and healthcare facilities. We are not an ambulance or emergency medical service.

Do you provide transportation for seniors?

Yes. We serve seniors who need dependable transportation to appointments, errands, family visits, senior programs, and other approved destinations.

Can a family member or caregiver arrange transportation?

Yes. A family member, caregiver, case manager, facility, or authorized representative may submit a request on behalf of the passenger.

Can I schedule recurring rides?

Yes. Recurring transportation may be arranged for work, dialysis, therapy, medical appointments, senior programs, or other ongoing needs, subject to availability.

Do you provide airport transportation?

Yes. Airport trips should be scheduled in advance. Customers should provide their airline, flight number, departure or arrival time, passenger count, and luggage details.

Do you provide out-of-town transportation?

Yes, depending on availability. Long-distance and out-of-state trips require advance scheduling and a customized quote.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on mileage, pickup and drop-off locations, trip timing, number of stops, passenger needs, waiting requirements, and whether the trip is one way or round trip.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Customers should contact Leeway Rides as soon as possible. Cancellation or no-show charges may apply depending on timing and circumstances.

Do you accept emergency transportation requests?

No. Leeway Rides does not provide emergency medical transportation. Call 911 for emergencies.
