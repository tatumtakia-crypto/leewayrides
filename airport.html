Airport Transportation Services

Leeway Rides offers scheduled airport transportation for individuals, families, business travelers, and visitors who need dependable, stress-free transportation to or from the airport. Please schedule your airport transportation in advance whenever possible.

Our Airport Services Include:

  • One-way and round-trip airport transportation
  • Early-morning and late-evening pickups
  • Transportation from a residence, hotel, office, or other approved location
  • Airport arrivals and departures for Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT), Greenville-Spartanburg (GSP), and regional airports
  • Transportation for business and personal travel
  • Out-of-town airport transfers
  • Scheduled return transportation coordination
  • Assistance coordinating pickup times based on flight schedules
  • Comfortable accommodation for passengers traveling with luggage
