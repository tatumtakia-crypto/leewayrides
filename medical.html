Medical & Senior Transportation

Leeway Rides provides professional, reliable, non-emergency transportation for ambulatory passengers who do not require emergency medical care or specialized medical equipment during transport.

Important Medical Disclaimer: Leeway Rides provides non-emergency transportation and is not an ambulance service. If a passenger is experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.

Services May Include Transportation To & From:

  • Primary-care and follow-up appointments
  • Oncology and dialysis treatments
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation facilities
  • Dental appointments and outpatient procedures
  • Pharmacies, hospitals, and medical centers
  • Senior centers, assisted-living communities, and adult day programs
  • Behavioral-health appointments and routine wellness visits
  • Family visits and personal errands

Program Highlights:

  • One-way and round-trip recurring appointment transportation
  • Scheduled pickup and return trips designed for seniors and ambulatory passengers
  • Seamless coordination with family members, caregivers, facilities, case managers, and authorized representatives
  • Advance scheduling and courtesy communication regarding pickup details
  • Door-to-door transportation when appropriate and safely available
  • Long-distance medical transportation available by customized quote
